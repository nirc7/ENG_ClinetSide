4. Formatted output - predefined font sizes:




Your first mark up tag is an <hNumber > tag.
This tag is called a heading tag. Therefore we use the letter h as tag.  The text written between opening and closing heading tag is marked.
Legal sizes are in range: 1-6.
<h1> denotes a largest heading and <h6> denotes the smallest heading.
