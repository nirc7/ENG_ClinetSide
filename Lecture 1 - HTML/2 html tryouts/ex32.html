32. Hypermedia - mailto:

mailto:someone@somewhere is an instruction that allows user to send an email from current web page.

Do not forget to put colon (:) sign between mailto and email address. Be careful with white spaces. If you put a white space before email address then email address will start from this white space.

In current example we created an email link on the webpage.
